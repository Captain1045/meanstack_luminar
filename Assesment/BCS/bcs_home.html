‘BUS CONCESSION SYSTEM’ facilitates more automation of various processes in the current concession system.The proposed system focuses on converting the entire manual processes into computerized operations. Computerized system will reduce the workload of the officers as it will make the applications for concession and pass online. It also results in easy storing of the details of the applicants in a database. The backup of data will prevent the data loss in any critical situation. It also facilitates in calculating the fare, expiry date, amount received etc. The proposed system will make available information sorted in any manner faster.
